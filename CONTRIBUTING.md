# Contributing to Frigate

To provide protections against bad actors/spam accounts. The Pull Request feature and direct commiting of files onto the repository is only available for authorized users.

Instead, you should use GitHub's Issues feature to propose a suggestion or report a bug. **AI Aided reports/suggestions are discoraged and also prohibited without permission from contributors.**

## Bug Reports

Your bug report should be detailed and provide a concise explaination on what the issue is and how to reproduce it.
You are also advised to provide a reproduction file to help reduce triaging times.

Some code hotspots that we recommend/greatly appricate review or ehancements on include:

- All Web API's (NeoShift, Technified etc.)

## Suggestions

Suggestion should provide a detailed explanation on what they intend on adding and what risks may be present.

Optionally stating alternatives/drawbacks to your idea is also greatly preferred (this is advised when collaborating with multiple users on your suggestion).
